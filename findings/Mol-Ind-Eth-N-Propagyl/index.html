Description
A novel indanyl‑propargylamine derivative developed by Nervorum Biotechnologies. The compound is a secondary amine bearing an indane moiety and an N-propargyl substituent. It is being studied as a potential monoamine oxidase (MAO) inhibitor with central nervous system (CNS) activity.

Applications may include treatment of neurological and neuropsychiatric conditions such as Parkinson’s disease, ADHD, and depression.
